Week 1 · Lake Geneva base

Day 1
Zürich arrival, train to Montreux. Lakefront settle-in, Belle Époque stroll.
Day 2–3
Lake Geneva Circuit. Train to Lausanne, CGN paddle steamer to Chillon, Lavaux wine terraces walk.
Day 4
Gruyères + Maison Cailler. Chocolate factory, cheese cellar, hilltop village. Day trip by train.
Day 5
Lavaux vineyards on foot. Long wine-terrace walk from St-Saphorin to Lutry. Market lunch.
Day 6
Down day in Montreux. Lakeside, Queen studio museum, casino if you're curious.
Day 7
GoldenPass Line to Interlaken. Scenic travel day, Belle Époque carriage.

Week 2 · Bernese Oberland base

Day 8
Interlaken settle-in. Walk the Aare, check out Höhematte park, plan the week.
Day 9
Jungfraujoch "Top of Europe". Cogwheel up, glacier experience, back by evening.
Day 10
Schynige Platte + Faulhorn walk. Cogwheel up, long panoramic ridge walk if weather.
Day 11
Lauterbrunnen valley. Waterfalls, Trümmelbach, maybe Mürren funicular.
Day 12
Rest day. Aare swim, read, farmer's market, Meiringen chocolate shop.
Day 13
Brienzersee boat + Rothorn Bahn. Steam cogwheel up the Brienzer Rothorn; panoramic summit.
Day 14
Train to Zermatt. Via Brig; afternoon walk along the Vispa.

Week 3 · Zermatt + Matter Valley base

Day 15
Gornergrat sunrise. Early cogwheel, Matterhorn light, breakfast up top.
Day 16
Five Lakes Walk. Classic 2–3h loop with Matterhorn reflections.
Day 17
Saas-Fee day. Neighboring village, glacier viewpoints, Ice Pavilion.
Day 18
Klein Matterhorn + Schwarzsee. Cable car to Europe's highest viewing platform.
Day 19
Down day + raclette lunch. Wander Hinterdorf (old village), shop for Valais chocolate.
Day 20
Findel Glacier trail + Riffelsee. Deeper hike into the Gorner region.
Day 21
Glacier Express to St. Moritz. The scenic travel day — or take regular trains and stop in Andermatt.

Week 4 · Engadin base

Day 22
St. Moritz + Muottas Muragl sunrise. Funicular up at dawn, long breakfast.
Day 23
Bernina Express day trip. Down to Tirano and back — or break the journey in Poschiavo.
Day 24
Val Fex walk. Classic Engadin high-valley hike from Sils.
Day 25
Val Müstair day. UNESCO Swiss National Park, Müstair monastery frescoes.
Day 26
Diavolezza high-alpine. Cable car, glacier panorama, short hike.
Day 27
Train to Zürich. Via Chur; evening dinner in Niederdorf, last chocolate stops.
Day 28
Depart Zürich.

Week 1 · German Switzerland

Day 1–2
Zürich. Altstadt, Kunsthaus, Sprüngli for Luxemburgerli, Uetliberg.
Day 3–4
Appenzell. The picture-book Switzerland — hand-painted houses, cheese dairies, Alpstein hikes up the Ebenalp.
Day 5–7
Lucerne. Lake steamer + Pilatus (Tell Route), Chapel Bridge, Lindt Home day trip.

Week 2 · Bernese Oberland + Zermatt

Day 8–10
Interlaken. Jungfraujoch, Schynige Platte, Brienzersee steamer to Rothorn Bahn.
Day 11–14
Zermatt. Gornergrat, Matterhorn, 5 Lakes Walk, Findel Glacier, day trip to Saas-Fee.

Week 3 · Alpine east + Italian Switzerland

Day 15
Glacier Express → St. Moritz. 8-hour classic.
Day 16–17
St. Moritz. Bernina Express day to Tirano, Muottas Muragl, Engadin walks.
Day 18–20
Lugano. Via Centovalli Express (Tirano → Locarno). Monte Brè, Bellinzona UNESCO castles.
Day 21
Gotthard Panorama Express → Bern. Scenic crossing back north.

Week 4 · French Switzerland

Day 22–23
Bern + Gruyères. Old Town UNESCO, Aare swim, chocolate at Maison Cailler, cheese in Gruyères village.
Day 24–26
Geneva. Old Town, Jet d'Eau, Lake Geneva Circuit (Lausanne + Chillon on a CGN steamer), Lavaux walk.
Day 27
Train to Zürich. Last-day Zürich (Bahnhofstrasse, chocolatiers, dinner).
Day 28
Depart Zürich.

Lucerne. Pilatus cogwheel (Tell Route), Vierwaldstättersee steamer, Lindt Home day trip.

Interlaken. Jungfraujoch, Schynige Platte ridge walk, Brienzersee + Rothorn Bahn.

Week 2 · Alpine anchors

Day 8–11
Zermatt. Matterhorn sunrise at Gornergrat, 5 Lakes Walk, Findel Glacier, Saas-Fee day.
Day 12
Glacier Express → St. Moritz. 8-hour scenic.
Day 13–14
St. Moritz. Muottas Muragl, Val Fex walk, Diavolezza glacier panorama.

Week 3 · Crossing into Italy

Day 15
Bernina Express → Tirano. Roofless open-air car if summer. Lunch in Tirano (you're in Italy).
Day 16–18
Lake Como. Base in Varenna or Bellagio. Ferries between villages, Villa Carlotta, mountain views.
Day 19–20
Milan. Duomo rooftop, Brera, Last Supper if you booked, aperitivo culture.
Day 21
Verona. Opera in the Arena if summer, Old Town stroll.

Week 4 · Venice finale

Day 22–25
Venice. Canals at dawn, Cannaregio walks, Burano colors, Rialto market.
Day 26
Dolomites day trip. Train to Belluno, a taste of the mountains before flying home.
Day 27
Venice last day. A morning, a farewell caffè, cicchetti lunch.
Day 28
Depart Venice (VCE).
